🧠 About Counseling

Counseling is a confidential and supportive service designed to help students manage academic, personal, emotional, and social challenges.

At LASU, our professional counselors are here to provide guidance, encouragement, and practical support whenever needed — no judgment, no pressure.

Why Seek Counseling?

  • 🎓 Academic stress or exam anxiety
  • 🤝 Relationship or family issues
  • 😔 Feelings of sadness, isolation, or low self-esteem
  • 🧭 Decision-making and goal setting
  • 😤 Anger or stress management
    • “Sometimes just talking to someone makes all the difference.”

What You Can Expect

Our counseling sessions are:

  • ✅ Private and confidential
  • ✅ Judgment-free
  • ✅ Conducted by trained LASU professionals
  • ✅ Tailored to your unique situation

Ready to Talk?

Whether you're going through something serious or just need someone to talk to, we're here. You can book an appointment or start an instant live session at any time.
